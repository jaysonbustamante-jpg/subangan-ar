Bridging the past
through augmented reality

Explore 3D digitized artifacts from Davao Oriental in AR or browse the archive gallery — no camera needed. Print the Hiro marker (tap View Marker), point your camera at it, and see history come alive.

Quick Start · AR Mode

  • 1

    Tap Start AR and allow camera access.

  • 2

    Point camera at the Hiro marker — tap View Marker to print it.

  • 3

    Touch gestures — drag to rotate, pinch to zoom, two-finger drag for height.

Archive Gallery Mode

Browse digitized artifacts without a camera. Tap Open Archive — then use touch gestures to interact: one finger to rotate, pinch to zoom, two fingers up/down for height.

AR Note: Tap View Marker to open the Hiro marker — print it and scan with Chrome (Android) or Safari (iOS).
