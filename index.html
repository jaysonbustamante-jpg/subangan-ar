Bridging the past
through augmented reality

Explore 3D digitized artifacts from Davao Oriental in AR or browse the archive gallery — no camera needed. Point your device at a marker to see history come alive, or discover artifacts at your own pace.

Quick Start · AR Mode

  • 1

    Tap Start AR and allow camera access.

  • 2

    Point camera at the Hiro marker or QR code.

  • 3

    The 3D artifact appears · use controls to rotate, zoom, adjust height.

Archive Gallery Mode

Browse digitized artifacts without a camera. Tap Open Archive to explore the collection — rotate, zoom, and read about each piece in an immersive 3D viewer.

AR Note: Works best on Chrome (Android) or Safari (iOS). Ensure camera permissions are allowed.
