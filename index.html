Subangan ARchive System

Marker-based AR archive prototype
Explore artifacts through AR

Scan a QR code to open this page, then tap Start AR to enable your camera. When the marker is detected, a 3D model appears on your screen.

Quick start

  • 1
    Tap Start AR and allow camera access.
  • 2
    Point the camera at the Hiro marker (printed or on another screen).
  • 3
    The 3D artifact appears on top of the marker.

Demo notes

Note: AR works best on mobile devices (Android/iOS). On laptop, camera may not start if another app is using it or permissions are blocked. Laptop testing: open this page in Chrome or Edge (not the VS Code preview), then tap Start AR and allow camera. If it fails, check Windows camera privacy settings and close other apps using the camera.
